Tenant Responsibilities

  • Sweep and vacuum daily
  • Participate in the Water Jug rotation (₱30). Notify the group chat once you’ve contributed. Jugs that needed to be replaced are brought near the fire exit beside the lobby desk. Make sure to click or visit the Rotation Page here.
  • Purchase medium-sized trash bags as needed and replace them once the bin is full.
  • Mop the unit at least 2–3 times a week.
  • Conserve electricity: Limit usage to two fans per room. Don't Leave any appliances unattended (it would result in a penalty)
  • Air-Con hours: 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM. Start at 18°C for 30 minutes, then adjust to 24°C for the remaining time.
  • Make sure to fix the sofa after using it.
  • Make sure to scrub and wipe the floor of the restroom after using it.
  • Don’t be messy, Don’t treat the condo as if you're the only one using it. Be Respectful at all times especially on the other tenants who are paying fair also.
