Visitors

These rules ensure safety, respect for shared spaces, and fairness among all tenants.

Visitor Guidelines:

  • Visitors must be registered at the lobby desk according to building policy. Please inform your visitor to provide your full name and unit number (2419) and their corresponding ID. Make sure to also inform your tenants and roommates via the group chat provided.
  • No visitors are allowed inside the unit after 10:00 PM. This is to maintain quiet hours and avoid noise complaints.
  • Overnight visitors are allowed. For Family and Relatives, you must give a letter of overnight stay to the Admin Office. For GF/BF/Spouse Make sure to update your corresponding tenants and roomates of their intent to stay for the night.
  • Visitors must stay in common areas only (Salas + Dining Area). Entry inside bedrooms is not allowed for security and privacy reasons.
  • You are responsible for your visitor’s behavior. Any mess, damage, or rule violation caused by your guest will be charged to you and counted as your offense and you will be penalized.
  • No loud conversations or gatherings, especially during quiet hours (11:00 PM). Applicable to Overnight tenants only. But you can tell the tenant with visitor if you want to have a peaceful day.
  • Visitors must follow unit rules regarding: — Cleanliness — Proper disposal of trash — Not leaving items unattended — No tampering with appliances — No smoking/vaping in the salas or shared areas

    • "Tenants are accountable for their visitors. Respect the shared home and each other."
