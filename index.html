Shared Spaces

What's available inside Unit 2419 for all tenants

Living Room (Salas)

  • Television available for shows & movies
  • Cozy sofa and yellow box chairs for your comfort
  • General table and dining table — available for studying; be mindful of other tenants
  • Board games available beside the TV for entertainment
  • Extra power ports on the extension cord
  • Clean carpets stored in black boxes near the TV
  • Chargers, extensions, hair curlers & hair straightener underneath the TV
  • Tools, tapes, zip-ties & accessories on the upper right corner of the TV

Dining Area

  • Plates, bowls, cups & utensils provided beside the dining table
  • Shelves with condiments, medicines, and storage space for food
  • Microwave, Air Fryer, 1 large & 1 small rice cooker available for use
  • Space beneath the microwave/air fryer can store shoes

Restroom

  • Equipped with shower, heater, toilet & sink
  • Shelves for personal toiletries
  • Shower dividers for privacy
  • Toilet paper beside the bowl; liquid hand soap beside the sink
  • Brushes, mops & plunger beside the bowl

Kitchen

  • Induction cooker, rice cooker, microwave, air fryer & sink
  • Water jugs shared rotationally among tenants
  • Cleaning supplies (Zonrox, trash bags) in last cabinet above refrigerator
  • Strainers & extra pans in the middle cabinet; knives beside the sink
  • Trash bin under the sink; drying rack beside the sink

Note: Do not dispose of food or liquid waste in the kitchen bin.

Tenant Responsibilities

Each tenant is expected to uphold these standards daily

  • Sweep and vacuum daily — keep common areas clean at all times
  • No smoking or vaping in the salas — do it in your room with roommate's consent
  • Participate in rotations — notify the group chat once done. (See Rotations)
  • Mop the unit at least 2–3 times a week
  • Conserve electricity — max 2 fans per room; no unattended appliances (see Penalties)
  • A/C Hours: 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM. Start at 18°C for 30 min, then adjust to 24°C. Set timer to 8 hours (max 10 hrs/day)
  • Fix the sofa after using — keep it tidy for everyone
  • Scrub and wipe restroom floor after every use to prevent accidents
  • No personal items left overnight in common areas — failure leads to a penalty
  • Be respectful — this is a shared home, treat it as such
  • No politics, no arguments — resolve issues promptly via group chat

"We do not tolerate poor hygiene or unclean habits within the premises."

Note: Failure to maintain cleanliness will result in multiple and severe penalties posted in the group chat. Funds go to cleaning materials.

House Rules

Rules that keep our shared home clean, safe, and peaceful

General Cleanliness

  • Always keep the Kitchen, Salas, and Restroom clean, clutter-free & organized
  • No smoking or vaping in the salas — go to your room with roommate's consent
  • If caught not doing assigned tasks after being reminded, a Penalty Fee applies
  • Quiet Hours: 11:00 PM — to avoid noise complaints and let others rest

Note: If you're absent, ask someone to cover your task, then return the favor on their assigned date.

Salas Rules

  • No personal items left in common areas — everyone shares this space
  • Keep the area clutter-free; clean up before you leave
  • Unplug the TV, fan, or extension cord when leaving for extended periods
  • Fix the sofa and pillows after use to maintain cleanliness

Restroom Rules

  • Scrub the floor, door & surrounding walls — mandatory everyday to avoid mold
  • Keep the area dry — floor and toilet bowl must be dry after use
  • Store personal items on designated shelves
  • 11:00 PM nightly check — failure to comply results in a penalty

Kitchen & Dining Table

  • Return all appliances to their proper places after use
  • No personal belongings left overnight on the kitchen or dining table
  • Wipe the sides and table after use — applies to both kitchen and dining table
  • Wash dirty dishes immediately — do not leave them unattended
  • Put leftover rice & bones in a strainer then in a separate plastic bag — not the bin, to avoid clogs. Remove food waste from the strainer before overnight
  • No dishes left overnight — all must be washed and stored before end of day

"We do not tolerate poor hygiene or unclean habits within the premises."

Note: Penalties will be posted in the group chat for transparency and will be used for cleaning materials & maintenance.

Visitor Rules

Ensuring safety, respect, and fairness for all tenants

📌 Tenants are accountable for their visitors at all times
  • Register visitors at the lobby desk — provide your full name, unit (2419), and visitor's ID. Inform housemates via the group chat.
  • No visitors inside the unit after 10:00 PM — to maintain quiet hours and avoid noise complaints
  • Overnight stays allowed — for family/relatives, submit a letter to the Admin Office. For GF/BF/Spouse, inform your housemates via group chat
  • Visitors must stay in common areas only (Salas + Dining Area) — bedrooms are off-limits for security and privacy. Exemption: relatives or partners
  • You are responsible for your visitor's behavior — any mess, damage, or rule violation will be charged to you as your offense
  • No loud conversations or gatherings after 11:00 PM quiet hours
  • Visitors must comply with unit rules on cleanliness, trash disposal, appliance use, and no smoking/vaping in shared areas

"Respect the shared home and each other."
